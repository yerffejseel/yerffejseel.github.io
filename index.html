Spring 2025

Research Collaborator, Apple

With Dr. Chun-Liang Li, in CSE 493

Researched vision-centric test-time scaling for vision–language models using Visual Sketchpad–inspired augmentations: depth maps, segmentation masks, and crops. Built an experimental pipeline on LLaVA-OneVision and MMBench to evaluate three augmentation strategies, then a hybrid system that pairs GPT-4o-mini augmentation planning with LLaVA-OneVision inference as a lighter-weight alternative to full Visual Sketchpad methods.

The hybrid gave a small accuracy gain (+1.37% on the benchmark) at roughly 33× lower inference cost. I wrote up the work as a short paper.
